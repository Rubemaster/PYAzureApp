Schedule 2 of the GDPO sets out the scope of permitted development rights.; The swept area of any building mounted wind turbine blade must be no more than 3.8 square metres.; The highest part of the stand alone wind turbine must not exceed 11.1 metres.; The swept area of any stand alone wind turbine blade must be no more than 3.8 square metres.;Full details about these permitted development rights are set out on the Governments Planning Portal website.
