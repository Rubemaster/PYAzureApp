Smidth Aeromotor with threebladed rotor (rotor diameter 24 m, rated power approx. 70 kW), 1942 5;In Germany, the first attempts of using wind power for the generation of electricity go back to before World War I. Some companies, Kster and Hercules among others, manufactured American wind turbines under license. Until the thirties, a total of 3600 wind turbines were built in Germany by about ten manufacturers. Most of these were used for pumping water, their intended purpose, but some of them were modi fied for the generation of electricity.
