Cost of equity  Based on the feedback of one interviewee, they use the Capital Asset Model (CAPM) and a more aggregated approach compared to the theoretical model conducted. Specifically, they mainly take into consideration  in an aggregate way  the risk profile of the asset according to the (i) technology, (ii) regulation  market and (iii) country risk. Therefore, they do not individualise premiums per specific risks as presented above. Specifically, for the case of the market premium, a range between 56 is assumed. In total, an average cost of equity of 11 is considered. Another expert stated that this value shows fluctuations from company to company (e.g. companies with great and diverse portfolio, small scale companies).
