Based on the estimations of cost of equity and cost of debt, the Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) can be estimated12. This ratio is important, as it gives insights in the total costs of project funding from both equity and debt. To estimate the WACC, the cost of equity and cost of debt are needed and the ratio between them. This debtequity ratio is estimated based on observations of recently developed projects and is for all Member States set at 7030, meaning that 70 of the projects is funded with debt and 30 with equity.
